Why Our School Needs Longer Recess

I think our school should make recess longer. I have three reasons why this
is a good idea.

First, kids need to move their bodies. In the article it says that children
should get sixty minutes of exercise every day. Right now we only get
fifteen minutes of recess. That is not enough time to run and play a whole
game of tag or soccer.

Second, recess helps us learn better. When we sit too long my legs get
wiggly and I stop listening to my teacher. After recess my brain feels
awake. The article also says that students pay attention better after they
play outside.

Third, recess is when we make friends. At recess I met my best friend
because we both like to jump rope. You cannot talk to your friends during
math class, so recess is the best time for that.

Some people say longer recess means less time for learning. But I think we
would learn more because we would not be so tired and bored.

In conclusion, our school should make recess longer because our bodies need
exercise, our brains work better after we play, and friends are important
too. I hope our principal reads this and changes the schedule.
